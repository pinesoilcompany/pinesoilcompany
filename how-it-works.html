Our Process

How It Works

Simple steps. Serious impact. No guesswork.

01
📋

Schedule a Pickup

It starts with a simple call or online form. Tell us your location, the type of oil, and an approximate volume. We'll match you with a pickup slot that fits your schedule — often within 24–48 hours. No contracts required for first-time clients.

  • Online booking or phone scheduling
  • Available Monday–Saturday
  • Commercial and residential accepted
  • No minimum volume for first pickup
02
🚛

We Collect It

Our trained crew arrives at your location with proper equipment to safely extract and transport your used oil. We handle all types — cooking oil, motor oil, hydraulic fluid, and more. Every drop is documented for compliance and traceability.

  • Licensed and insured drivers
  • SPCC-compliant spill prevention
  • Collection receipts provided
  • Tank cleaning available on request
03
⚗️

Refine & Process

Back at our local processing facility, oil is filtered, dewatered, and converted through transesterification into high-quality biodiesel. Byproducts like glycerin are responsibly processed — nothing goes to landfill.

  • ASTM D6751 standards met
  • B100 and blended grades produced
  • Closed-loop waste management
  • Quality tested before distribution
04
🌍

Renew the Cycle

Our biodiesel hits the road — powering local fleets, farms, and generators. Clients receive optional impact reports showing the CO₂ offset generated by their contributed oil. One gallon of used oil = roughly one gallon of clean biodiesel.

  • Impact reporting for partners
  • Certificates of recycling issued
  • Biodiesel available for purchase
  • Continuous collection cycle

Common Questions

Quick answers.

Is the pickup really free?
Yes — for qualifying volumes. We'll confirm during scheduling. Most commercial clients qualify automatically.
What types of oil do you accept?
Used cooking oil (UCO), motor oil, hydraulic oil, gear oil, and most petroleum-based waste oils. Contact us if unsure.
How quickly can you schedule a pickup?
Typically within 24–48 hours. Same-day service is available in select areas — just ask when you book.
Do I need a contract?
Not for one-time pickups. For recurring programs we offer simple service agreements with flexible terms.
