Service 02

Biodiesel Production

Used cooking oil and certain grades of waste petroleum oil can be converted into biodiesel — a clean-burning, renewable fuel. Pines Oil Company processes eligible feedstocks through transesterification to produce quality biodiesel suitable for diesel engines and heating systems.

This process captures value from material that would otherwise require costly disposal, while contributing to a cleaner fuel supply chain. It is one of the most practical forms of industrial recycling available today.

  • — Feedstocks include used cooking oil and select petroleum oils
  • — Output used in diesel engines and industrial heating
  • — Reduces reliance on virgin petroleum inputs
Service 03

Recycling Services

Not all collected oil becomes biodiesel. Some material is processed and re-refined for industrial lubricant use, used as fuel at permitted facilities, or directed to the appropriate downstream handler based on its content and grade.

Pines Oil Company ensures that every gallon collected is managed responsibly — no dumping, no illegal disposal, no shortcuts. We maintain full chain-of-custody records and can provide documentation for your compliance files on request.

  • — Re-refining into base oil lubricants
  • — Fuel blending for permitted industrial burners
  • — Full documentation provided for your records
