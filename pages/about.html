About the Company

East Texas Biodiesel.
Built From the Ground Up.

Pines Oil Company is an independently owned biodiesel production operation rooted in East Texas agriculture and community. Here is our story.

Founder and Operations Lead
Brant Borden
Founder and Operations Lead, Pines Oil Company

Brant has always been interested in entrepreneurship, but especially entrepreneurship that helps his community. Born in Mesquite, Texas, Brant visited family in East Texas frequently, so when his family moved to Hideaway, Texas, he quickly fell in love with the East Texas life. Throughout his 7 years of life in Hideaway and the surrounding cities, he's made friends and connections in the community. Hearing the people around him complaign about fuel prices, Brant wished he could do something about it. One day when Brant was suprisingly listening to his chemistry teacher, he was introduced to the 'transesterfication' process. Diving into the science behind the topic, Brant was deeply intrigued, and after experimenting in his parents' garage, he realized he could help people with this new interest.

The idea behind Pines Oil Company was simple, collect resturaunts' waste cooking oil, and turn it into biodiesel for local farms and ranches. This business model was exactly what Brant was looking for. His interest in business conjoined with his love to help others.

Our Values

What Drives This Company

01

Local First

Every gallon of oil we collect comes from East Texas restaurants. Every gallon of biodiesel we produce stays in East Texas. The supply chain is local to support the community around us.

02

Straightforward Business

We pick up oil for free. We process it and sell fuel. We do not overcomplicate the business model. Simplicity and reliability are how we build trust with partners on both ends of the chain.

03

Agricultural Respect

We understand that farmers and ranchers need dependable, fairly priced fuel. We approach every buyer relationship with that understanding and with a commitment to straightforward dealing.

04

Operational Integrity

From collection to processing to delivery, we operate with transparency. Restaurant partners know where their oil goes. Buyers know where their fuel comes from. Accountability matters to us.

05

Long Term Thinking

Our partnerships with local restaurants and farm buyers are the foundation of what this company is and what is to come.

06

East Texas Identity

This company was built in East Texas for East Texas. Our processing plant is in Athens, our feedstock comes from Lindale and Van, and our customers are our neighbors.
