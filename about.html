Our Story

About Pines Oil Company

A grounded company with a straightforward mission: handle used oil the right way, every time.

Company Overview

Who We Are

Pines Oil Company is a used oil collection and recycling business serving commercial and industrial clients throughout our region. We were founded on a simple principle: used oil is too valuable — and too hazardous — to be handled carelessly.

A single gallon of improperly disposed motor oil can contaminate up to one million gallons of fresh water. That fact drives everything we do. We built this company to make compliant, responsible disposal easy for businesses that generate used oil as part of their everyday operations.

We are not a large corporation. We are a locally operated business that takes its work seriously — showing up on time, handling your materials properly, and giving you the documentation you need to stay in compliance.

Our Mission

What Drives Us

Our mission is to make used oil recycling accessible, dependable, and fully compliant for every business we serve. We believe that environmental responsibility should not be a burden — it should be a standard part of how businesses operate, made easy by the right partner.

We take pride in knowing that the oil we collect does not end up in a ditch, a storm drain, or a landfill. It becomes biodiesel. It becomes base lubricant. It becomes something useful. That is the outcome we work toward every day.

How We Operate

Our Core Values

  • Reliability

    We show up when we say we will. Our clients depend on us to keep their operations moving and their facilities compliant. We do not miss scheduled pickups.

  • Transparency

    Every collection is documented. You know exactly how much was picked up, where it went, and how it was processed. No black boxes.

  • Compliance

    We operate within all applicable EPA, state, and local regulations governing used oil collection and handling. Your business is protected by working with us.

  • Environmental Stewardship

    Every operational decision we make is filtered through one question: does this protect or harm the environment? We consistently choose the former, even when it costs more.

  • Simplicity

    We keep things simple for our clients. You generate oil, we collect and process it. The paperwork, the logistics, the downstream handling — that is our job, not yours.

  • Local Focus

    We are a locally operated company. We know our region, our clients, and our regulatory environment. You are not dealing with a national call center.

Our Commitment

We handle your oil like it matters — because it does.

Improper used oil disposal is one of the most preventable sources of environmental contamination in the country. Every gallon we collect and recycle is a gallon that does not end up somewhere it should not be. That is the work we are here to do.
