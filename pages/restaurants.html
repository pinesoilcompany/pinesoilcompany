The Program

A Better Solution for
Your Waste Oil

Every commercial kitchen generates used cooking oil. Disposing of it properly costs time and money, and improper disposal creates real liability. Pines Oil Company eliminates that problem for restaurants across the Lindale and Van, Texas area.

We provide scheduled, reliable collection of your used fryer oil and cooking grease. You maintain your normal kitchen operations. We handle the rest, at no cost to your business.

Your waste oil does not go to a landfill or down a drain. It goes to our processing plant in Athens, Texas where it becomes biodiesel that fuels local farms and ranches. You play a direct role in that supply chain.

Accepted Materials

What We Collect

Used Fryer Oil

Spent vegetable oil, canola oil, soy oil, and other oils from commercial deep fryers. This is the primary feedstock for biodiesel production.

Cooking Grease

Animal fats and mixed cooking greases generated during food preparation. These materials are also suitable feedstocks for our processing operation.

Yellow Grease

Previously used cooking oil and grease that may contain food particles. We accept this material from grease trap collections where applicable.

If you are unsure whether your waste oil qualifies, contact us. We are happy to discuss your specific situation before beginning any pickup arrangement.
