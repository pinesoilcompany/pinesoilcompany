Our Process

From Restaurant Kitchen
to Fuel Tank

Every gallon of biodiesel we produce starts as used cooking oil collected from restaurants in the Lindale and Van areas. We transport it to our Athens facility, process and refine it, and deliver a finished fuel product to East Texas agricultural operations.

About Our Operation
01

Collection

We schedule regular pickups from partner restaurants in Lindale, Van, and surrounding East Texas communities. Pickup is always free of charge.

→
02

Transport

Collected oil is transported to our processing plant in Athens, Texas, where it undergoes quality intake inspection and storage.

→
03

Processing

The raw oil is filtered, processed through transesterification, and refined into off-road biodiesel that meets specification for agricultural and industrial use.

→
04

Distribution

Finished biodiesel is made available to farmers, ranchers, and other qualifying buyers across East Texas for use in off-road diesel equipment.

About the Company

Rooted in East Texas,
Built to Last

Pines Oil Company was founded on a straightforward premise: East Texas restaurants produce thousands of gallons of waste oil every year, and East Texas farms burn thousands of gallons of diesel. We bridge that gap.

Our processing plant in Athens, Texas handles every step of production in-house. We collect, process, and distribute locally, keeping resources and economic activity within our region.
