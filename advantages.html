Why Pines

The Smarter Choice.

What sets us apart from the alternative.

Most businesses treat used oil as a liability — something to pay to dispose of, a compliance headache, a waste stream without value. We see it differently. Used oil is a resource, and we've built a company around unlocking it.

Our Advantages

Every reason
to switch.

💰

No Cost to You

Qualifying clients pay nothing for pickup. We offset our costs through biodiesel production — your waste creates value that covers the service.

⚡

Fast & Flexible

24–48 hour scheduling. Same-day where possible. We adapt to your operations, not the other way around.

📄

Full Compliance

We handle the paperwork. Every pickup includes compliant documentation, collection receipts, and certificates of recycling for your records.

🌿

Real Environmental Impact

Every gallon collected prevents contamination and offsets emissions when converted to biodiesel. We provide impact reports so you can see the difference.

📍

Locally Operated

We're not a national chain. We're your neighbors — East Texas-based and community-invested. Faster service, personal relationships, shared stakes.

🔄

Closed-Loop System

Nothing we collect ends up in a landfill or waterway. Every drop is processed, recycled, or responsibly repurposed — a true circular model.

🤝

Long-Term Partnership

We're not a one-time service. Recurring clients get dedicated scheduling, priority access, volume bonuses, and a direct line to our team.

🏷️

Transparent Pricing

No hidden fees. No surprise charges. We quote clearly before any service, and our payment terms are straightforward.
